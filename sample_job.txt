Job Title: Marketing Coordinator

We are seeking a Marketing Coordinator to join our growing team. The ideal candidate will support marketing campaigns, analyze performance data, and assist with content creation across digital channels.

Responsibilities:
- Coordinate and execute marketing campaigns across email, social media, and web
- Analyze campaign performance using Google Analytics and provide insights
- Manage content calendar and collaborate with design team
- Conduct market research to identify trends and opportunities
- Support SEO and paid advertising efforts

Requirements:
- Bachelor's degree in Marketing, Communications, or related field
- 1-2 years of experience in marketing or related role
- Proficiency in Google Analytics, Canva, and social media management tools
- Strong written and verbal communication skills
- Experience with email marketing platforms (Mailchimp, Constant Contact, etc.)
- Basic understanding of SEO principles
